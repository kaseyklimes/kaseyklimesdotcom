In 2013, following Mayor Bloomberg's proposal to rezone East Midtown, Gehl Architects created a public space improvement strategy. The initiative aimed to enhance pedestrian experiences amid increased urban density.

I served as data specialist, contributing analytical work and visualization design for the district's public realm framework.
